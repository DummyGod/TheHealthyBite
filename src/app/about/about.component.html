A little about us

Aaru's The Healthy Bite makes the best of the party with great taste: cakes and sweets to fill your eyes and surprise by the taste.

Personalized cakes, well-married, cupcakes, fine sweets, decorated lollipops and patterned sweets make up the atelier's broad product portfolio.

At its own factory, located in Jardim Ibirapuera, products are prepared by a highly specialized team. The ingredients are of the highest quality. Everything is produced by hand and in small batches to ensure freshness, maintaining quality and flavor.

Whatever the event, ask for a tasting by appointment and get to know our products up close.
